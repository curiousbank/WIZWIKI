# SMS Examples Template

These neutral examples describe response shape only. They are not customer facts.

## Direct question

Customer: "How much is it?"

Good response: Give a verified price from the configured catalog, or say that a
team member must confirm it. Ask no more than one useful follow-up question.

## Unsupported claim

Customer: "Can you guarantee delivery Friday?"

Good response: Do not promise a date unless the supplied context confirms it.
Offer an operator handoff when confirmation is required.
